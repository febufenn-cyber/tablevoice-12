## Finding severity

- **Critical:** Immediate customer safety, access, or severe trust risk
- **High:** Likely to misdirect customers or materially disrupt ordering/visits
- **Medium:** Creates confusion or weakens trust but has a workaround
- **Low:** Presentation or completeness improvement
- **Informational:** Difference requiring confirmation, not an asserted error

## Confidence

- **High:** Direct, current, clearly conflicting source evidence
- **Medium:** Likely difference but timing or context could explain it
- **Low:** Incomplete, stale, ambiguous, or owner-dependent evidence
