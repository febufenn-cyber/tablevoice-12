# Outreach Messages

Use only evidence visible for the specific restaurant. Do not claim revenue loss, wrong information, or recurring complaints unless the source supports it.

## Message A — Unanswered reviews

Hi {{name}}, I noticed {{restaurant}} has {{count}} recent customer reviews without a public reply. I prepared a few draft responses in a tone suitable for the restaurant, plus a short reputation snapshot. You approve everything; nothing is posted automatically. May I send the one-page snapshot here?

## Message B — Listing inconsistency

Hi {{name}}, while checking {{restaurant}} I found that {{field}} appears differently on {{source_a}} and {{source_b}}. That can confuse customers, although the correct value needs your confirmation. I prepared a short listing-health snapshot showing this and a few other checks. May I send it?

## Message C — Recurring theme

Hi {{name}}, several recent reviews for {{restaurant}} mention {{theme}}. I summarised the pattern and prepared safe draft replies plus one suggested internal action. I can share the short report; nothing requires account access or automatic posting.

## Message D — Time-saving angle

Hi {{name}}, I’m testing Tablevoice with independent restaurants. It turns each review into: what happened, what the restaurant should check internally, and a ready-to-approve public reply in the restaurant’s own tone. For the pilot, replies arrive through {{channel}} and you publish them manually. Would a 14-day test be useful?

## Follow-up after snapshot

The most important finding appears to be {{finding}}. The next step is not an app installation: we can run a 14-day concierge pilot, prepare the replies, flag sensitive cases, and send one weekly action report. You retain final control and publish manually.

Pilot option: {{offer_name}} at {{price}} for {{duration}}.

## Non-response follow-up

Hi {{name}}, closing the loop on the {{restaurant}} snapshot. The finding about {{finding}} still needs owner confirmation, so I have not labelled it an error. I’ll close the report unless review or listing support is currently a priority.

## Referral request

Thank you for completing the pilot. Is there one restaurant owner or agency operator who deals with the same review/listing problem and would give honest feedback? A direct introduction is more useful than a public endorsement.

## Guardrails

Never say:

- “You are losing ₹X” without defensible evidence.
- “Your listing is wrong” when it is only inconsistent.
- “We reply automatically” during Phase 0.
- “We remove negative reviews.”
- “Google/Zomato has authorised Tablevoice.”
- “AI handles every complaint.”

Track the exact message variant, response, snapshot acceptance, pilot conversion, and payment outcome.
